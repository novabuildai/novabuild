Getting Started

ATARA helps you discover balance and wisdom by tracking your emotions, guiding you with affirmations, and connecting you to both ancient and modern paths of insight. Choose your journey — Mystic, Manifestor, or Crown — and start logging your emotions and vibes to unlock daily wisdom.

Common Questions

How do I log my emotions?

Open ATARA and tap “Check In Now.” Select the emotion that best describes how you feel. ATARA will record your vibe and provide you with a tailored affirmation or wisdom quote.

What does “vibe frequency” mean?

Your vibe is a numerical reflection of your emotional state. Over time, ATARA graphs your check-ins so you can see patterns and track your personal growth.

Can I switch my chosen path?

Yes. You can switch between the Mystic Path, Manifestor’s Path, and Crown Path anytime in your account settings to explore different styles of wisdom.

What kind of wisdom will I receive?

ATARA draws on ancient texts, modern philosophy, and curated affirmations. Each check-in provides quotes and insights matched to your selected path and emotional state.

Troubleshooting

  • If the app is not responding, try restarting ATARA or your device.
  • Ensure you have the latest version installed from the App Store.
  • Check your internet connection, as ATARA requires access to sync your data.
