What is PL8?

PL8 is your AI-powered kitchen assistant. It helps you track your pantry, generate recipes with the ingredients you already have, and plan meals that match your dietary preferences and lifestyle.

Key Features

  • Smart Pantry: Scan, snap, or add ingredients manually to keep track of what’s in your kitchen.
  • Recipe Generator: Instantly get recipes tailored to your available ingredients, dietary preferences, and serving sizes.
  • Personalized Suggestions: Discover unique and trending recipes curated just for you.
  • Shopping Integration: Easily add missing items to your grocery list and (soon) order directly with supported retailers.
  • Seamless Sync: Access your pantry and recipes across devices — never lose track of what you have.

Why Choose PL8?

With PL8, meal prep becomes stress-free and fun. No more food waste, no more “what should I cook tonight?” — just personalized cooking made simple.
